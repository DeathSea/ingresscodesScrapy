Code #1

Observations

25 characters, notice the range and what is missing from the range.

Solution

Usually letters ranging from a to j would be a convert to numbers that would give you 0 to 9. Notice in this case that there are no a, b or i but there are a few 1 and 2 ? Changing these to roman numeral will give you a few i and an even number of characters to have good Ascii range.
