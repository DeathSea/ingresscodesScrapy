Code #2

Observations

Letters and numbers. Now this one is truly different.

Hint
You don’t see this cipher very often. A mix of letters and numbers might hint to Base64 but that would be half right :p

Solution

Thanks to 井伊狐狸（EcoliW3110）for this solve.

None of the usual cipher gave anything good. A mix of numbers and letters could indicate Base64 but in this case there are no modifiers to let you know which letters should be caps. Ah but there is Base32 that uses only capital letters and numbers from 2 to 7.

Convert from Base32 to Hex

CA6CDFA2EB1EBEB79C85AAE07A78A79E29EC8B16DB

Doesn’t look like good hex range but if you convert that to Base64 you get
