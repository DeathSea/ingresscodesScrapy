Code #1

Observations

16 characters with the right amount of numbers for a passcode format.

Solution

Make a 4×4 grid

6 5 s h
k 3 v a
v 3 e p
y 6 d e

Read in a spiral from Y

yvk65shaped633ve

Code #2

Observations

20 characters, that same old cipher will help you get it if you can spot the keyword. And no tada is not a valid keyword !

Solution

Make a 4×5 grid

w w 1 0
i 5 v i
t a d a
i i 2 v
o r i 6

In that middle row, can you see the keyword ? Data, reorder the columns this way: 3, 4, 1, 2

1 0 w w
v i i 5
d a t a
2 v i i
i 6 o r

You now have

10wwvii5data2viii6or

Convert 10 to roman numeral x and other roman numerals to numbers

xww75data286or

Code #3

Observations

14 characters, everything seems to be there but out of order

Solution

You can see the keyword have that make up the ends of the string. Simply start reading from y

yrk78have857rz
