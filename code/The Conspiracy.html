Code #1

Observations

All numbers, try to find the pattern

Solution

Let’s start by making pairs to see what we are working with

20 32 35 00 09 11 24 32 38 39 37 28 23 26 07 08 08 12 32

Definitely not numbers to letters. Where the numbers should fall in the passcode format, numbers are all below 10. If you try to add 50 to all numbers you will get close to a good Ascii range but not quite. You need to add 5 to all the digits

75 87 80 55 54 66 79 87 83 84 82 73 78 71 52 53 53 67 87
