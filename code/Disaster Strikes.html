Code #1

Observations

Exactly 4 numbers in the string, however it does not match the passcode pattern.  There is also a possible keyword substitution in the center of the string.

Solution

There are two substitutions that need to be performed here:

  1.  The letter in the prefix, “u”, needs to be replaced with a word that would fit the format of the prefix letters.  The word “you” is the substitution.
  2. “Nomad” is also not a known keyword and must be substituted.  A google search will reveal that the Niantic character Dr. Henry “Hank” Johnson is the star of the archeology based reality series, NOMAD.  The keyword “hank” is the substitution.

Finally, the first letter in the suffix is obtained through a fairly recent technique in which the last letter of the keyword section is used twice.  In this case, we keep the “d” from “nomad.”

5you3hankd4k7w
