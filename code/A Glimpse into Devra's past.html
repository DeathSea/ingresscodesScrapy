Code #1

Observations

16 characters with the right amount of numbers for a passcode. You know what to use.

Solution

Make a 4×4 grid

f u 2 g
u 3 r p
e s 4 u
k 4 n 7

Nothing much to get a code. Looking at the letters you can see the keyword pursue. Reorder the columns like so: 2, 4, 1, 3

u g f 2
3 p u r
s u e 4
4 7 k n

ugf23pursue447kn

Code #2

Observations

31 characters. A mix of numbers, lower case letters and only one upper case letter.

Hint
Upper & lower case and numbers, rings a bell? Things are just not what they seem

Solution

With numbers, lower and upper case letters, Base64 has to be a good candidate.

Let’s split the string to make it more clear

rf 03 lod 03 dqvjstA 23 msv 06 25 n 10 07 g

Convert lower case to uppers case, numbers to letters (a=0) and uppercase to numbers (a=0)

RFdLODdDQVJST0xMSVgzNkhG

Convert Base64 to text

DWK87CARROLLIX36HF
