Three codes were hidden in the HTML source of the Resistance claim #Ingress161210 post on the Investigate Ingress blog.

[1] g7oyecd7t2ieyt66

[2] 10r658s7eybiywq49vpb

[3] KO?&!FH BE%A!”$F=

Code #1

Observations

16 characters with the right amount of numbers. You’ll need to spot the keyword to get everything in order.

Solution

Let’s try to get the keywords. Using Regex Keyword Finder  we can use ^[g7oyecd7t2ieyt66]{6}$ to get the keyword Deceit.

Make a 4×4 grid

g 7 o y
e c d 7
t 2 i e
y t 6 6

You can see where the letters of the keyword are, you’ll need to rearrange the columns as 4, 3, 1, 2

y o g 7
7 d e c
e i t 2
6 6 y t

yog77deceit266yt

Code #2

Observations

20 characters. Try to spot the passcode format then you’ll have to do the same with what’s at the end of your fingertips.

Solution

Looking at the string we see somewhat of the passcode format written in reverse. Let’s reverse the string.

bpv94qwyibye7s856r01

Not much of a keyword, Atbash and Rot don’t give anything to work with. That 01 at the end gives a good hint. It should be a letter, if you use a keyboard flip it becomes 10 which can be converted to roman numeral X. Let’s try a keyboard flip: 1=0, q=p, a=;, z=/

nqm27potenti4l365u10

Convert 4 to A from leetspeak and 10 to X from roman numeral

nqm27potential365ux

Code #3

Observations

Yeah well it’s that kind of string that you look at and just have to play around to see what ranges you get. If you see something familiar but with a few typos you are on the right track.

Solution

After trying a few thing I converted the string to decimal

75 79 63 38 33 70 72 32 66 69 37 65 33 8221 36 70 61

Few things are popping out. First those 38 & 33 at position 4 & 5 indicate that the range can be Hex. That 8221 is obviously off, simply change that right quotation mark to a regular quotation mark

KO?&!FH BE%A!"$F=

Convert Ascii to decimal

75 79 63 38 33 70 72 32 66 69 37 65 33 34 36 70 61

Convert Hex to Ascii

uyc83pr2fi7e346pa

We clearly have a good prefix and suffix. For the keyword, not sure if it’s a typo in the code or if there is a special manipulation to get it right but we can figure out that profile is the right keyword

uyc83profile346pa
