Code #2

Observations

We see numbers and letters.  Ranges seem limited and some combinations seem to repeat.

Hint
It’s not hex.  Note the limited range of numbers and letters. Except for the 5’s, we can group in pairs.  What do you notice about the value of the pairs.
Solution

We see that some pairs repeat.  Keep the 5 separate, and group the rest into pairs.  5 is lonely.

5 b3 a4 5 a4 b3 b3 2c 5 1d b3 2c c2 5 a4 a4 a4 d1 c2 2c c2 4a c2 d1 5 b3 a4 d1 5 a4 c2 c2

Notice how each pair ‘adds’ up to 5.  What systems have groupings of 5 things?  Numbers in Morse.
