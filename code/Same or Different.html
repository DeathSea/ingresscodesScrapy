Code #1

Observations

15 characters with the right amount of numbers for a passcode.

Hint
That same old cipher applies but you’ll need to figure out the keyword to get everything in the right order
Solution

Make a 3×5 grid

c e 4
6 v q
u 4 h
7 a b
k 2 l

Nothing much of a passcode at first sight but try to look for a five letter keyword that would fit in that middle column. Equal is the keyword. Let’s shift row 2 left by 1, row 3 right by 1 and row 4 left by 1

c e 4
v q 6
h u 4
7 a b
2 l k

Read down for the upper left C
