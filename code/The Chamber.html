Code #1

Observations

All letters with no particular range.

Solution

After trying most of the commom cipher, nothing came out. Converting letters to numbers didn’t give anything either until I looked at the a=25 z=0 result which is basically an Atbash

Atbash the string
ldbobpafgafvplgajhajikfkikflgmkfdafdafhajhle

Convert letters to numbers using a=0
11 3 1 14 1 15 0 5 6 0 5 21 15 11 6 0 9 7 0 9 8 10 5 10 8 10 5 11 6 12 10 5 3 0 5 3 0 5 7 0 9 7 11 4

We now have 57 characters, group them in 3
113 114 115 056 052 115 116 097 098 105 108 105 116 121 053 053 057 097 114

Now that looks like decimal to Ascii

qrs84stability559ar

Code #2 & #3

Observations

Both codes are the same length and work together.

Solution

The first string is reversed so reverse it and put one string on top of the other

vcy69mutn493fg
bcw39meoa749ho

Read in a zigzag pattern alternating between the two codes

vcy39muon799fo
