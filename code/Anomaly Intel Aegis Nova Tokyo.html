Codes #1

Observations

Right passcode format, go through your usual cipher routine.

Hint
dots and dashes manipulation
Solution

Atbash and ROT didn’t give anything good. Next one on the list is Morse

-.- . .-- .---- ---.. -.. -. -.- ----. .---- ....- --.- .-.

Swap morse codes

.-. - -.. -.... ...-- .-- .- .-. ....- -.... ----. ..-. -.-

Convert morse to text

RTD63WAR469FK

Invalid. Swap morse is like Atbash, the reverse of war is peace

rtd63peace469fk

Code #2

Observations

Same amount of letters and numbers
Thanks goes out to yushi2000 for submitting the solve for this one!

Hint

You’ll need to start at the center of it all

Solution

For a 4×4 grid of the code

2 6 s 1
8 c s 5
d 9 s r
y 9 j 4

Start reading the code from the bottom right s in the the middle, spiraling anti-clockwise outwards:
ssc99j4r51s628dy
Replace the numbers 5 and 1 with their Roman Numbers V and I,
at the same time replace the number 4 with it’s L337 representation of A
ssc99jArVIs628dy
and there we have our final code! A bit trickier than normal, but still a fun one to solve!
