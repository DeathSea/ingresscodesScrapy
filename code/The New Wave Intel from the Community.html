Code #2

Observations

Again, all the right ingredients. Try to spot the keyword to help you rearrange everything

Solution

Make a 2×10 grid

s g d a w 7 6 2 9 4
p s e i r l s o e p

Rearrange columns to get keyword persepolis and the right prefix and suffix

s d w 6 9 4 2 7 a g
p e r s e p o l i s

sdw69persepolis427ag

Code #3

Observations

28 letters, numbers and symbols. Notice how symbols are always repeating.

Solution

This is Base64, here’s how

First convert lowercase to uppercase

QUF!!M&&ZGVU5DVE((PT&&Y3OE9H

Remember a few days ago we had a cipher that used symbols to specify the keyboard row and letter numbers. Those symbols are exactly that. !! is first letter of second row (a) && is 7th letter of second row and (( is 9th letter of second row. This gives us the needed lowercase letters to finish the base64 string

QUFaMjZGVU5DVElPTjY3OE9H

Convert Base64 to text

AAZ26FUNCTION678OG
