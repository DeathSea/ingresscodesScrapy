Code #1

Observations

15 characters with the right amount of numbers for a passcode. Same old cipher…

Solution

Make a 5×3 grid

v v 2 t 3
l d y a b
8 6 x 6 n

You can see the keyword dalby in the middle row. Reorder the column as 2, 4, 1, 5, 3

v t v 3 2
d a l b y
6 6 8 n x

Code #3

Observations

16 characters with the right amount of numbers for a passcode. Same old cipher but with a different twist.

Solution

Make a 4×4 grid

c 6 e a
8 v j 2
t s m h
5 u t 7

The only possible keyword would be statue. Shift up column 2 by 1, 3 by 2 and 4 by 1

c v m 7
8 s t a
t u e 2
5 6 j h
