Code #1

Observations

26 digits. Take a good look at the distribution.

Solution

Let’s make pairs to see what we are working with.

38 24 44 54 54 35 41 32 53 57 58 26 42

Look at the where the numbers positions would be in the passcode, all in the 50s which is good for Ascii. Atbash those numbers

72 86 66 56 56 75 69 78 57 53 52 84 68

Convert decimal to Ascii

HVB88KEN954TD

Code #2

Observations

16 characters with the right amount of numbers for a valid passcode.

Solution

Make a 4×4 grid

9 u 3 f
w e l o
p n a 5
z 2 r 7

Read going up from the lower left Z

zpw92neural375of
