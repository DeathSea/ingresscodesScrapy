Three codes where found hidden in the The #Saveklue Resurgence post in the Investigate Ingress blog.

[1] b9stfj8aa6ft2aeu

[2] nrmeklylbibkrdunlixmxeihct

[3] nriieseingeikeapoa

Code #1

Observations

All the ingredients are there, the regular manipulation will help you get it.

Hint
Round and round we go !
Solution

4 numbers and the rest are letters, surely it’s a transposition cipher. Make a 4×4 grid

b 9 s t
f j 8 a
a 6 f t
2 a e u

Different twist on how to read the code. Read in a spiral going up from the bottom left 2

2afb9statues6j8f

Code #2

Observations

All letters, go through your usual cipher routine

Hint
Need to practise reading backwards
Solution

After ROT and Atbash didn’t give anything try skip. Look carefully at a skip of 2

nmkybbrulxxicrellikdnimeht

Doesn’t look like much at first glance but try reading backwards, can you see The Mind Killer ?

Let’s split the string in 2 and reverse the last part

nmkybbrulxxic

themindkiller

The mind killer refers to fear which is a valid keyword

Let’s use Vigenere autokey

Passphrase: fear

Message: nmkybbrulxxic

Result: iikhtthnseqvk

That gives

2kht3n7q5k

Simply insert the keyword between the prefix and suffix to get

2kht3fearn7q5k

Code #3

Observations

Again, all letters, go through the usual ROT, Atbash, Transposition routine

Solution

Guess Niantic was out of ideas for cipher, we have yet another transposition.

Make a 6×3 rectangle

n r i i e s
e i n g e i
k e a p o a

Not seeing much of a code? Old tick, reverse the second row

n r i i e s
i e g n i e
k e a p o a

Read down from the upper left n

nikreeigainpeiosea

9kre8gainp8o7a
