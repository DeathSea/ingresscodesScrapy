Code #1

Observations

16 characters with all the right ingredients for a passcode.

Solution

Make a 4×4 grid

p 3 a 4
f h d y
4 e s p
h 4 p 6

Reverse rows 1 & 3

4 a 3 p
f h d y
p s e 4
h 4 p 6

Read going up from the lower left H

hpf44shaped364yp
