Observations

Looks like groups of 4 but that would mean that the delimiter is changing value between / and , which doesn’t really make sense.

Hint
Mirror mirror on the wall…
Solution

This type of cipher as been used a lot in the past but having those / and , at exactly those emplacements is really mean. Looking at your keyboard will give you the answer, if you have a QWERTY-layout keyboard. For those that don’t have one, you can use this image in the meantime.
