Code #2

Observations

47 letters, go through your usual cipher routine to find the cipher

Solution

Atbash gives

lxvslxxviitlxxxivilivplulivlliialtlxviiiilxxiio

Looks like roman numerals with some extra characters. Let’s split things up

lxv s lxxvii t lxxxiv i liv p l u liv l lii a l t lxviii i lxxii o

Convert roman numerals to numbers and isolate the extra letters

65 77 84 54 50 54 52 50 68 72

stipulatio

Convert decimal to Ascii

AMT62642DH

Stipulatio is the basic form of contract in roman law

amt62contract642dh
