Code #1

Observations

The range of some numbers will quickly guide you to the cipher

Solution

Let’s start by making pairs to see what we are working with

15 28 38 18 24 47 34 22 36 47 36 26 18 24 26 34 33 34 32 38

Look at the range of the first digit in every pairs: 1 – 4. This is a Phone cipher. For each pairs, the first digits indicates the amount of presses of the second digit to get the corresponding letter
