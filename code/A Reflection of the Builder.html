Two codes could found hidden in the A Reflection of the Builder post on the Investigate Ingress blog.

[1] tso22genafcbfr196lh

[2] UsMeh^w@n*dlEkTqIuSnAlRtAePf#o*rQdg5Zm0hWr

Code #1

Observations

Passcode format is there, go through your regular cipher routine.

Solution

Rot 13 reveals the code. Remeber that you have to do the same with the numbers.

gfb99transpose863yu

Code #2

Observations

A good mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers & symbols. Remember that there are usually 3 passcode per posts.

Solution

Looking at the middle of the string you can see the word PARASITE written backward. Let’s isolate the capital letters and symbols

UM^@*ETISARAP#*QZW

Change symbols to their respective numbers on the keyboard

UM628ETISARAP38QZW

Reverse

WZQ83PARASITE826MU

Now let’s take what was left of the original string, lowercase letters and numbers

sehwndlkqunltefordg5m0hr

We have 24 character of which two are numbers. This could be Base64 but we need to find what letters are caps and which are lowercase.

All letters should be changed to capital letters except for those that are preceded by a letter that makes up the Base64 string.

Ex:

Original string

UsMeh^w...

Would give

SEhW

The final Base64 string is

SEhWNDlKQUNLTEFORDg5M0hR

Convert Base64 to text

HHV49JACKLAND893HQ
