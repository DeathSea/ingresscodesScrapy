Code #1

Observations

12 characters with the right amount of numbers for a valid passcode. There aren’t many 2 letter keywords

Solution

A quick search for 2 letter keywords reveals that the keyword should be Ni

Make a 6×2 grid

4 n t 4 g n
q c 6 6 i 9

Reorder columns as 5, 6, 3, 4, 1, 2

g n t 4 4 n
i 9 6 6 q c

gnt44ni966qc
