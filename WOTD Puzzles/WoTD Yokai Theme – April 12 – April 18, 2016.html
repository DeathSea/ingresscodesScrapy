Observations

Am I pretty? Ociobyjqjche
Do you need your legs? Kinyvi

Not very much to go on here, but garbled text normally hints to come transformation cipher.

Hint
When Atbash and ROT fail, there is always one more that can take a passphrase!

Solution
Firstly we need to figure out what/who the questions are referring to, so lets Google them.
Since we are in a Yōkai theme, include “Yōkai” in the search criteria to make finding them faster.
Am I pretty? is said by Kuchisake-onna
Do you need your legs? is said by Teke teke
I want to drown you but you are bowing? is said by Kappa
Perform a Vigenére on each line of garbled text with the name of the Yōkai referenced
Ociobyjqjche > Eightgjgfour
Kinyvi > Reduce
Ctwgeokudubj > Sthreekfourj
Adding it all together, we get
EightgjgfourReduceSthreekfourj
Substitute the numbers for the final code
8gjg4REDUCEs3k4j
