The first phrase seems a bit confusing.
It means that you must read the code in a spiral pattern
attazk(ks)attahawezniujsjauw

Looking up norse numbers, atta=8, niu=9, sjau=7
8zk(ks)8hawez9j7w

The (ks) produces the sound x.
Looking at the keyword, the w needs to be replaced by a v to get have:
8zkx8have9j7w

Still invalid. Few possibilities, the k could be q and/or the w could be a v.
Using brute force to get a valid password, the k needs to be changed to q:
8zqx8havez9j7w
