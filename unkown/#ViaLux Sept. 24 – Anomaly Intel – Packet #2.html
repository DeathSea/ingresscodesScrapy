Code #1

Observations

16 characters with the right amount of numbers for a passcode.

Hint
Same old recipe with extra mixing
Solution

Make a 4×4 grid

v u g t
f 7 o 5
4 l 3 e
x h n 4

Nothing really good, even by reordering the columns. You can see the keyword could be hulong if you reorder the rows. Reorder in this way: 2, 4, 1, 3

f 7 o 5
x h n 4
v u g t
4 l 3 e

Read down from the upper left F

fxv47hulong354te

Code #3

Observations

35 characters including numbers, capital and lowercase letters.

Hint
You know one cipher that uses numbers, capital and lowercase letters. Things are just not what they seem

Solution

Numbers, capital and lowercase letters has to be Base64. We’ll need to do some manipulation with those numbers. Let’s split everything to make it easier to read

25 NLL 13 Z 25 J 24 22 bW 24 M 21 SB 03 08 Z 12 d 09 V

Looks like numbers to capital letters, capital letters to lowercase and lowercase to numbers
